A Performance Joke

So a person goes to see a therapist. He sits down in the therapist's office,
gets comfortable, and proceeds to explains that lately he's been
overwhelmingly depressed. His job requires him to move around a lot,
he often has to work nights, and this has put strain on his marriage.

The therapist listens carefully, before saying, "Y'know, I think I have
just the thing for you. Tonight, the clown The Great Pagliacci is performing
and you should go see him if you have a chance. I'm sure that will cheer you right up."

The man looks at the therapist despairingly and moans:

"But doctor, I am ________"
