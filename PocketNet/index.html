Thank you for participating in PocketNET™'s internal app review process. By assisting in our app review process, you are contributing to a rich and vibrant community of software developers and users engaged in meaningful interactions every day. Please refer to the instructions below to begin your review session. Thank you!

Instructions

  1. Please begin by opening the following links in new tabs. They will provide you with the materials for conducting the review session.
    • Apps to be Tested
    • PocketNET™ Developer Terms of Service
    • Review Form
  2. Take some time before beginning the test to review our app store terms of service. This will provide important points to look for when evaluating candidate apps.
  3. For each candidate app provided to you to review, follow the instructions on the "apps" page to download it and set it up on your personal device.
  4. Spend fifteen(15) minutes using each app in as many ways as you deem possible.
  5. After using each candidate app for at least fifteen(15) minutes, use the provided evaluation form page to submit your evaluation of each candidate app.

Thank you for your participation!
