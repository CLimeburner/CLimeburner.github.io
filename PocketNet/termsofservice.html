3 PROHIBITED CONTENT

3.1 The kinds of content described below are strictly prohibited on the POCKETNET™ app store.

3.2 PROHIBITED CONTENT

3.2.1 Content that is harmful, depicts harm, or advocates for harm is prohibited. This includes, but is not limited to:

a) Depictions, both real and fictional, of physical or psychological harm being inflicted upon humans. This is exceptionally permitted for valid news organizations.

b) Depictions, both real and fictional of physical or psychological harm being inflicted upon animals. This is exceptionally permitted for valid news organizations.

c) The advocation of, or depiction of activities that are likely to result in grievous bodily injury.

d) Abusive speech or speech that advocates harm against a specific individual. This is exceptionally permitted for cases of legitimate satire.

e) Abusive speech or speech that advocates harm against a specific group based on sex, sexuality, gender identity, race, age, religion, culture, or political affiliation. This is exceptionally permitted for cases of legitimate satire.

f) The discrimination or advocation of discrimination of a specific group based on sex, sexuality, gender identity, race, age, religion, culture, or political affiliation. Discrimination may include:

(i) Refusal of services.

(ii) Exclusion from specific spaces or activities.

(iii) Erasure from historical or public records.

(iv) Speech that asserts a hierarchy amongst categories of the above-mentioned groups.

g) Depictions of substance abuse or the use of illicit substances.

h) Speech that advocates for substance abuse or the use of illicit substances.

3.2.2 Content that is sexual in nature is prohibited. This includes, but is not limited to:

a) Depictions of the genitalia and anus of either a man or woman, or depictions of breasts. This is exceptionally permitted for cases of legitimate art.

b) Depictions or descriptions of sexual acts.

c) Content that is deemed exploitative or pornographic.

d) Content that objectifies a specific individual.

3.2.3 Content that encourages children to engage in reckless financial behaviour is prohibited. Software that includes this content must provide suitable means of age-verification to ensure children are not exposed to it without parental consent. This includes, but is not limited to:

a) Gambling or gambling-like mechanics (poker, slot machines, loot boxes).

b) Glorified depictions of gambling.

c) In-app purchases or micro-transactions.

d) Links to third-party sites where purchases or micro-transactions maybe conducted.

3.2.4 It is strictly prohibited for software in the POCKETNET™ app store to make use of intellectual property (copyrighted, trademarks, or patents) they do not own. This includes, but is not limited to:

a) Use of POCKETNET™’s logo, branding, trademarks or derivative logos, brandings, and trademarks, with the exception of the “Available on POCKETNET™” advertisement seal.

b) Use of copyrighted, trademarked or patented material as content.

c) Reference to copyrighted, trademarked, or patented material deemed to violate the rights of the material’s owner. This includes, but is not limited to still images, videos, branding identity, audio, and quotation.

3.2.5 Software that is intended to mislead users or otherwise spread misinformation is prohibited with the exception of legitimate satire. This includes, but is not limited to content regarding:

a) Current events

b) Politics

c) Religion

d) Medical affairs

e) Individuals’ reputation (slander, libel)
